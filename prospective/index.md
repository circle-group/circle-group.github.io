# {% include icon.html icon="fa-regular fa-user-school" %}Prospective Students

## Applying as a PhD Student

PhD applications at Imperial College London are centralized. After applying at the University, those who pass the initial screening process are forwarded to the potential supervisors. If you wish to join the CIRCLE team, mention 'Tolga Birdal' as your supervisor in the formal application. More information can be found <u><a href="https://www.imperial.ac.uk/computing/prospective-students/phd/">here</a></u>. Imperial provides <u><a href="https://www.imperial.ac.uk/computing/prospective-students/courses/phd/scholarships/">a variety of scholarships</a></u> and the choice will vary depending on the candidate. Please consider applying for CDTs as well.

In general, we have multliple <u><a href="https://tolgabirdal.github.io/assets/pdf/circlejobs_phd.pdf">PhD positions</a></u> available. 

## Applying as a Postdoctoral Resarch Associate (PDRA)

We have multiple <u><a href="https://tolgabirdal.github.io/assets/pdf/circlejobs.pdf">PostDoc positions</a></u> available. It is recommended to contact Tolga Birdal in advance, so that your application is not missed.
