<strong>Zhengrui Xiang</strong>  hails from Sichuan, China. He completed his BSc degree in Information and Computing Science at the Beijing Institute of Technology, followed by an MSc in Statistics at Imperial College London. Later, he earned another MSc in data science and machine Learning from University College London. His current research interests lie in general generative model theory, and their applications on 3D, multimodal data.
