<strong>Gabriel Nobis</strong> is a PhD student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Fraunhofer HHI. He studied mathematics and computer science at the Technical University of Berlin with focus on stochastic processes and machine learning. His primary research interests lie in the study of fractional noise for generative modelling and the application of diffusion models in the life sciences and the medical field. Gabriel visited CIRCLE in 2025 working on generative models driven by fractional Brownian motion.
