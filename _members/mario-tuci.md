<strong>Mario Tuci</strong> is a joint PhD candidate in Mathematics at ENS Paris, Inria and Computing at Imperial College London. Previously, he completed his Masters and Bachelors degrees at the Mathematics Department of the Technical University of Munich in Germany. His work involves theoretical investigations of deep neural networks, particularly generalization.
