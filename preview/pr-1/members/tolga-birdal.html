As a computer scientist, I strive to understand visual perception and build machines that can see. To do so, I leverage tools from differential geometry and algebraic topology, in conjunction with deep neural networks. My research focuses on:

  • 3D Computer Vision
  • 3D/4D Generative Priors
  • Geometric/Topological Deep Learning
  • Statistical and Topological Learning Theory
  • Quantum Computer Vision

As a science advocate, I am committed to breaking down barriers to education, making knowledge accessible to all—especially those from underrepresented and underprivileged communities. Through initiatives like Bilimler Koyu and Nesin Maths Village, I actively contribute to this mission. I envision the possibility of an alternative, transformative academia. #baskabirakademimumkun

As an entrepreneur, I have co-founded multiple startups, including BeFunky, an online photo editing platform that empowers creativity worldwide.

As a music aficionado, I am immersed into drums & percussion. I particularly appreciate blues, jazz, funk as well as folk, ethnic and experimental forms of rhythm and music holistically.
